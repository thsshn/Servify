About Servify
What is Servify?

A smarter way to find and offer services across Malaysia.

Servify is a Malaysian service marketplace that connects people who need services with skilled professionals who provide them. Whether you need a caterer for your event, a cleaner for your home, a photographer for a special occasion, or a tutor for your kids — Servify is the one place to find them all.

We built Servify because finding a reliable, trustworthy service professional in Malaysia was unnecessarily hard. Too many scattered platforms, too many unverified listings, and too little accountability. We're fixing that.

Every provider on Servify goes through a real sign-up process, and every interaction is designed to be fast, simple, and stress-free. We're just getting started — and we'd love you to be part of it.
